Fried eggs

instructions

  • Hit up the frying pan
  • pour some oil
  • let it hit up a little
  • brake the eggs and pour into the frying pan
  • wait for 3mins and sprinkle some salt
  • rotate the egg onto the other part
  • wait up for 4mins and take off the fire
  • put it on a plate
  • serve
